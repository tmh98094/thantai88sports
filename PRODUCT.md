# Thantai88sport Product Context

## Product

Thantai88sport is the sports-focused satellite site in the Thantai88 ecosystem. It publishes Vietnamese sports news context, football education, pre-match reading guides, and responsible 18+ platform-navigation content.

Primary ecosystem relationship:

- Main hub: `https://thantai88.online` for general iGaming education, trust, account, promotion, safety, and ecosystem pages.
- Sports satellite: this repo, focused on sports intent and sports betting education.
- Slots satellite: standalone slots education, slot-category pages, and responsible-play content.

## Audience

- Vietnamese-speaking adults aged 18+.
- Readers who want sports news context, football guides, and safer decision-making before moving to a partner gaming platform.
- Beginners who need clear explanations of match context, bankroll limits, odds-reading concepts, and responsible-play habits.

## Brand

- Energetic sports editorial voice.
- Direct, practical, and trust-first.
- SEO-aware, but never visibly “SEO stuffed.”
- CTA-prioritized, but every CTA should feel responsible and age-gated.

## Content Rules

- Vietnamese only for visitor-facing copy.
- No guaranteed-win, risk-free, sure-bet, fake odds, fake live-score, fake license, fake payout, or unsupported platform/provider claims.
- Sports API data may inform drafts and reports, but published pages require human review.
- News APIs are for topic discovery only; do not copy or summarize full third-party articles as original reporting.
- Partner-platform evidence from `thantai688.com` may be used only at the category/lobby level unless the client provides confirmed provider/game data.

## UI Principles

- Homepage should move from hero → fresh posts/news → platform context → SEO authority categories → responsible CTA.
- Use sports green as the base color, with lime/gold accents for activity and conversion.
- CTAs should be clear, visible, and repeated, but not predatory.
- Sticky CTA must remain hideable and reopenable with a visible edge tab.
- Mobile copy must stay readable; avoid overly tight letter-spacing on Vietnamese headlines.

## Anti-References

- Generic betting landing pages that overpromise wins.
- English/internal labels in visitor-facing copy.
- Unsupported “best odds,” “highest payout,” or “hot today” claims.
- Pages that bury 18+ / responsible-play context below aggressive CTAs.
